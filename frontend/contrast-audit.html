Summary of Changes

  1. Replace hardcoded #5B8DEF with var(--color-primary-500) so light mode gets #1C39BB (5.5:1)
  2. Use primary-500 for links/interactive text instead of primary-400 (3.6:1 → 5.5:1)
  3. Use accent-700/800 for functional yellow text & icons in light mode (1.9:1 → 4.8:1+)
  4. Keep bright yellows decorative only — #FFB800 is fine as a background fill, just not for text/icons on white
  5. Increase glass opacities — glass-bg: 0.04→0.08, glass-border: 0.1→0.2, overlays: 10%→15-18%
  6. Scrollbar thumb — primary-300→primary-400 (meets 3:1 for non-text UI)
